Facebook - freedom
I developed this little extension for Safari for all the people who are addicted to Facebook. Whenever browsing to Facebook, freedom blocks the content and shows a blue screen instead. It tells how long it has been since the last Facebook visit. If you really need to gain access to Facebook you have to click a button.
The plugin is still under development and I'm not responsible for bugs associated with the downloadable preview.
